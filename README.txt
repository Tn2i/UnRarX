INSTALLING
----------
Open up a terminal.
do: cd PATH_TO_UNRARX_DIRECTORY
do: sh build.sh

Wait until the build is done, then open PATH_TO_UNRARX_DIRECTORY/build in the 
Finder. Drag UnRarX to your Applications folder, or wherever you want it.
